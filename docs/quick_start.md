# Scientific Pro Calculator — Quick Start

Get up and running in 5 minutes.

## Steps

1. **Install & Launch**
   - Tap the **Scientific Pro Calculator** icon on your home screen
   - The app opens to the Calculator screen
   ![Step 1](screenshots/step_1.png)

2. **Enter Your First Calculation**
   - Tap numbers and operators to build an equation: `2 + 3 * 4`
   - Watch the **live preview** below the input field show intermediate results
   ![Step 2](screenshots/step_2.png)

3. **Get Your Result**
   - Tap the **Equals (=)** button
   - The result appears: `14`
   - (The app respects order of operations: 3 × 4 = 12, then 2 + 12 = 14)
   ![Step 3](screenshots/step_3.png)

4. **Explore Advanced Features**
   - Tap the **Graphs** tab to plot functions (e.g., `sin(x)`)
   - Tap the **Units** tab to convert between 250+ units (e.g., kilometers to miles)
   - Tap the **Statistics** tab to analyze data
   - Tap the **Constants** tab to access 90+ physical constants
   ![Step 4](screenshots/step_4.png)

5. **Customize Your Experience**
   - Tap **Settings** to change:
     - Display format (fixed, scientific, engineering)
     - Angle unit (degrees, radians, gradians)
     - Theme (light or dark)
     - Input mode (Infix or RPN)
   ![Step 5](screenshots/step_5.png)

6. **Save & Recall**
   - Tap **History** to see all past calculations
   - Tap a calculation to recall it
   - Tap the **Star icon** to add frequently-used items to **Favorites**
   ![Step 6](screenshots/step_6.png)

7. **Export & Share**
   - After a calculation, tap **Export** to save as PDF or image
   - Tap **Share** to send via email, messaging, or cloud storage
   - Export graphs, history, and datasets in multiple formats
   ![Step 7](screenshots/step_7.png)
