## 1. Introduction

Scientific Pro Calculator ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

## 2. Data Collection

### What We Collect

Scientific Pro Calculator collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Calculation History**: Records of all calculations you perform, including the equations entered, results, timestamps, and format settings used.
- **Saved Graphs**: 2D and 3D function definitions, parameters, axis ranges, and visualization settings for graphs you create and save.
- **Saved Matrices**: Matrix data, dimensions, and operations you save for later use.
- **Favorites & Quick Access**: Functions, constants, and unit conversions you mark as favorites.
- **Statistical Datasets**: Data tables and datasets you input for statistical analysis.
- **Undo/Redo History**: Records of editing steps within calculation sessions.
- **App Settings**: Your preferences for display format, themes, input mode (Infix/RPN), decimal places, digit separators, haptic feedback, and full-screen mode.

**The App does NOT collect:**
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, location)
- Device identifiers or tracking cookies
- Biometric data
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Information about your device or network

### How Data is Collected

All data listed above is collected **passively** as you use the App's features. No external servers, cloud services, or third-party platforms are contacted.

## 3. Data Storage & Security

**All data is stored locally on your device** using the App's internal database and file system. We do not transmit, upload, or sync your calculations, graphs, or settings to any remote server or cloud service.

**Security measures:**
- Data is stored in the App's private directory on your device, protected by your device's OS security.
- No encryption is applied to local storage (encryption is your device's responsibility).
- If you export data (JSON, CSV, or PDF), that exported file is stored wherever you choose on your device or cloud storage.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Scientific Pro Calculator does **not** integrate with or call any external APIs, cloud services, or third-party platforms. All calculations, graphing, and data processing occur entirely within the App on your device.

**No third-party services are used:**
- No analytics platforms
- No cloud storage
- No advertising networks
- No external math engines or APIs

## 5. Data Sharing

**Your data is NOT shared with anyone.** The App does not share, sell, or disclose your calculation history, graphs, datasets, or settings to:
- App developers
- Third-party companies
- Advertisers
- Analytics providers
- Any external parties

The only exception is when **you explicitly export and share** your data:
- If you export calculations as PDF/CSV and share the file via email, messaging, or file storage, you are responsible for that sharing.
- The App itself does not initiate any sharing.

## 6. Data Retention

Data is retained on your device indefinitely until:
- You manually delete it (clear calculation history, delete saved graphs, etc.)
- You uninstall the App
- You clear the App's data via device Settings

## 7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

### How to Delete Your Data

**Delete specific data:**
- Open the App > History screen > long-press entries > Delete
- Open the App > Favorites screen > tap favorites > Remove
- Open the App > Settings > Saved Graphs > delete individual graphs

**Delete all app data:**
1. On your Android device, open **Settings**
2. Navigate to **Apps** or **Application Manager**
3. Find **Scientific Pro Calculator**
4. Tap **Storage** > **Clear Data** or **Clear Cache**
5. Confirm deletion

**Uninstall the App:**
- All data associated with the App will be deleted from your device.
